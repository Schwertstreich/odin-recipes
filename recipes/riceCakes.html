Rice cakes

Description:

Sticky, delicious and gummy, rice cakes are a tasty and easy treat to make, be careful though, they can get quite addicting!

A superb snack food rice cakes have been becoming more and more popular due to their taste, simplicity and satisfying gummy texture. Follow our recipe to make some, and satisfy your family and friends!

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoon water warmed to between 105-115F
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup rice flour
